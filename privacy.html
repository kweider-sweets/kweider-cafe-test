Rewards Privacy Notice — Draft

This draft must be reviewed and completed before the loyalty programme is launched publicly.

Information collected

First name and phone number are required to operate the membership. Email address and birthday are optional. Marketing consent is optional and separate from membership.

How it is used

Membership information is used to identify the member, maintain points and rewards, prevent misuse, and provide account support. Marketing messages are sent only where a valid marketing choice applies.

Retention and rights

Define the retention period, contact address, legal basis, processors, access and deletion procedure, complaint route, and international transfers before launch.

This file is a development placeholder, not a final legal privacy notice.
